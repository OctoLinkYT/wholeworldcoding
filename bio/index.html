About Me

  • Amateur programmer focusing on frontent html and basic javascript. I'm working on learning python and php.
  • I try to make music, but I usually spend my time producing other music and creating the visual accompanyment for said music. You can see my own music and the music I've produced for my partner Buster on our professional arts channel, SPARK.
  • I'm working on my own animations. These animations will eventually be used in a full-fledged show that will again be on our professional arts channel, SPARK.
  • I release games and programs on my github, itch.io, and this website. These programs are under my main company, wholeworldcoding. Coding, however, is just the start of it. Learn more about wholeworldcoding on our homepage.

Projects

  • PlayMii - A daily livestream nearing 100 days of activity. The stream has created a tight community and introducted the concept of "collaborative games" that show off an individual's creativity and skill, all running solely off of youtube.
  • Galenite - A games site that is rich in features and plays games you never thought could be played in pure HTML. Made possible by a collaboration with QuietKid. The successor to Wholeworldcoding Games.
  • dotblank. - An interesting experiment in encoding that takes the input text and turns it into "nothing" - uses patterns of Tag Space emojis and actual spaces. They look identical, making a hidden and invisible message.
  • The Fallen - An experimental world comprised of games, animations, shows, and other forms of media. Known mainly for its associated ARG, KILLJOY. The channel linked here is more of a "hub" and there are a lot more features to this program on all of my various channels.
